Don't Throw Me Away

2D Cooperative Platformer (Custom Engine)

Don’t Throw Me Away is a 2D platforming game about flow and cooperation. The game emphasizes cooperation by balancing game mastery and difficulty to create a unique play experience. Two players will take control of simple geometric avatars that are linked together. Together, players journey through a minimalist and abstract world that will test their individual skill and teamwork. Each level of the game world will have a series of floating nodes that the players must string together. Activating all the nodes in a level allows players to progress through the game. In order to restore reach these nodes, players must utilize the dynamics of being tethered to one another. This bond will provide a framework for the movement system of the game. Players must tug, throw or swing each other to traverse the level. The level design of the game will reinforce the movement system by focusing on the bond the two players share and their discovery of the abstract world. Don’t Throw Me Away aims to explore methods of generating flow in a cooperative space.

Scorching Sea

Isomectric Real Time Strategy (Javascript)

Scorching Sea takes place on the ocean bed in a world that has no water left. Your party of 4 heroes venture to find the rumored endless water supply and save your home. You must manage your remaining water resources while surviving in a day and night cycle. You'll encounter many enemies on this journey and will have to face survivors and mutants. This game was made with a team of 4 programmers.

Boss Generator

Boss Generator and Analysis Tool (Python)

Built with a group of 3 in our Game AI course, we wanted to design a tool that creates bosses based on a player's toolset. Our design decisions were inspired by The Legend of Zelda Boss and Dungeon formula. In a Zelda game, you find a new item in a dungeon and that item is key in exploiting the upcoming boss. Our concept allows for the player to pick up any set of weapons they want and fight a generated boss. Since parameters are the player's toolset, every generated boss will require the player to strategize and utilize their tools in challenging but fair fights.

Baby Crisis

Interactive Narrative Game (Inform 7)

Baby Crisis is an interactive narrative told in the second person in which you have mysteriously shunk while babysitting your baby step brother. It is your goal to figure out how to return back to your normal size before your brother gets into any trouble. Navigate through your now maze-like house and find a solution to this predicament.
