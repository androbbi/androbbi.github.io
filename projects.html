Don't Throw Me Away

A 2D Cooperative Platformer built and designed with a custom engine

Don’t Throw Me Away is a platforming game about flow and cooperation. The game emphasizes cooperation by balancing game mastery and difficulty to create a unique play experience. Two players will take control of simple geometric avatars that are linked together. Together, players journey through a minimalist and abstract world that will test their individual skill and teamwork. Each level of the game world will have a series of floating nodes that the players must string together. Activating all the nodes in a level allows players to progress through the game. In order to restore reach these nodes, players must utilize the dynamics of being tethered to one another. This bond will provide a framework for the movement system of the game. Players must tug, throw or swing each other to traverse the level. The level design of the game will reinforce the movement system by focusing on the bond the two players share and their discovery of the abstract world. Don’t Throw Me Away aims to explore methods of generating flow in a cooperative space.

Scorching Sea

Real Time Strategy game where you seek for the endless water supply

Scorching Sea takes place on the ocean bed in a world that has no water left. Your party of 4 heroes venture to find the rumored endless water supply and save your home. You must manage your remaining water resources while surviving in a day and night cycle. You'll encounter many enemies on this journey and will have to face survivors and mutants. Scorching Sea is written in JavaScript.
